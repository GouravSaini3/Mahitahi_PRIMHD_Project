PROPOSAL · WHAT_CHANGES

From 281 names to 22 standard categories

The current list is replaced by the PRIMHD reference set, with nine PRIMHD categories left out of the standard list and the rest of today's variation absorbed into the 22 categories plus the free-text detail field.

WHY_IT_SHRINKS

Where the other 259 names go

The current list grew through free-text and duplication. near-duplicate pairs were the same organisation spelled differently, and most remaining names are specific providers — a single GP practice, marae, or NGO — that now live in the Referrer detail field under one of the 22 categories, rather than as separate picklist options. The Current RB list, Service config, and "Other" usage tabs hold the full evidence behind this.
