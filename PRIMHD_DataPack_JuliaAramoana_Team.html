D. Recommendation on Clinical Mental Health Service

Recommendation to remove Clinical Mental Health Service and utilise Clinical Template across services:

  • We recommend removing the Clinical Mental Health Service, as it is not the most effective way to provide clinical support. Maintaining it as a separate service can result in duplication of referrals, inflated referral counts, and unnecessary administrative work when the same referral needs to be entered from the main service into the Clinical Mental Health Service.
  • If clinical input is required, we can instead utilise the Clinical Template across all services to provide appropriate and consistent clinical support to Whaiora without creating additional referrals or administrative burden.

Your feedback will help ensure these updates align with operational priorities and compliance standards.

F. Next Steps After Review & Confirmation

Once we receive review and feedback from Directors and Branch Managers on the shared Data Pack, and confirm the PRIMHD activity codes for their services, we will move forward with proposing a new activity template design.

The proposed advanced activity template will:

  • Introduce guided data entry with built-in validation.
  • Ensure a clear distinction between Direct Contact and Attempt to Contact with Whaiora.
  • Provide context-appropriate field visibility.
  • Prevent invalid field combinations and minimise incorrect data entry.

This approach will streamline data capture, improve accuracy, and support consistent reporting across all services.
