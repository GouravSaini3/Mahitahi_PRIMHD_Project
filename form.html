Service Delivery Mapping Form

Use this form to define service activities, delivery pathways, outcome types, evidence requirements, and KPI measures. Complete one form per service, team, or programme.

1. Service Activities

List the main activities kaimahi deliver. Use plain service language, not reporting language. Choose the support type that best describes how the activity is normally delivered.

ActivityDescriptionSupport Type

Support Type Definitions

Immediate Need: Support delivered in response to an urgent issue requiring action within hours, days, or a few contacts.

Short Intervention: Targeted support delivered over a brief period, usually less than 12 weeks.

Ongoing Support: Support requiring multiple contacts and coordinated intervention over time.

Goal-Based Support: Medium to long-term change requiring planning, monitoring, and review.

Programme Support: Structured participation in a group, course, programme, or planned series of activities.

Clinical Support: Assessment, treatment, brief intervention, or other clinically led support.

Cultural Support: Support that strengthens cultural identity, whānau connection, whakapapa, tikanga, marae, iwi, hapū, reo, or other culturally grounded protective factors.

3. Outcome Mapping

Map each service activity or outcome area to immediate, milestone, and sustained outcomes. Do not overcomplicate this. Write what would be visible, recordable, or reported if progress occurred.

Outcome Definitions

Immediate Outcome: A change, achievement, or result that occurs directly after support. This may happen the same day or within a few contacts.

Milestone Outcome: A meaningful step toward a larger outcome. This shows progress but may not yet be the final result.

Sustained Outcome: A positive outcome maintained over time. This demonstrates longer-term change, stability, or continued engagement.

5. KPI Framework

Keep KPIs practical. For each activity, aim for one activity KPI, one outcome KPI, and one sustained KPI. The target should be clear enough to calculate from available data.

KPI Definitions

Activity KPI: Measures volume of service delivered, such as the number of interventions, sessions, contacts, referrals, or completed activities.

Outcome KPI: Measures whether support achieved the intended immediate or milestone result.

Sustained KPI: Measures whether positive change was maintained after a defined review period.
